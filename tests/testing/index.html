About Nuclei

My goal in creating Nuclei was to inspire and educate young minds about the fascinating world of computer science. As technology continues to advance, with developments like AI and Autonomous Driving, it is crucial for this generation to be equipped with the necessary skills to seamlessly integrate into the dynamic realities that await them. Even if they do not choose to pursue it as a career, I believe understanding the fundamental building blocks of computer science is a core in unlocking the hidden secrets that drive the many facets of this world, just like nuclei are to the core of atoms. With Nuclei, I hope to empower young people to explore and embrace this exciting field.
